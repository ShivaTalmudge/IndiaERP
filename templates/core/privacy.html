Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect company data, user credentials, and ERP records entered by you. We maintain a strict policy of zero data commercialization; we do not sell or lease your data to third parties.

2. Data Isolation

IndiaERP uses a multi-tenant architecture where each company's data is logically isolated. Staff members are cryptographically restricted to data belonging solely to their registered company.

3. Security

All sensitive information, including passwords, is hashed using industry-standard algorithms. Communication is secured via 256-bit SSL encryption to ensure integrity.

4. GST Data

GST-related data (GSTIN, invoice amounts, tax breakups) is stored securely and processed exclusively for your invoicing and compliance reporting needs.

5. Contact

For inquiries regarding your data or to request deletion, please contact our compliance officer at: privacy@indiaerp.in
